About DHARATI INFRASTRUCTURE

Dharati Infra is an excavation services provider based in Kolhapur, equipped with the most sophisticated infrastructure earthmoving as well as mining machinery in and around Maharashtra. For Over past 20 years the company has intensified providing various excavation and mining solutions to organizations all over the country setting up its terra firm amongst the business leaders. The company owns a wide range of heavy machinery and equipment that allows us to carry out projects with enough manpower in a precise stipulated time without compromising on the ensure effective management of client projects from proposal to completion. Additional machinery and extra supply of manpower at short notice due to our excellent relations with financial institutions and equipment manufactures to this date Dharati Infra is supplying machinery and manpower simultaneously to various projects justifying each and every client equally in terms work and time leading the company to exceed customer satisfaction. Dharati Infra is determined to ensure occupational health, safety and welfare of its employees considering the environmental peace in the company. Working with Dharati Infra we assure complete dedication and focused services as per customers demands.
